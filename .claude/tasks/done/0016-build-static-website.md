# Problem to solve

The website https://www.sambre-marne-yser.be/ is available under the form of Markdown and images.

The content of the website is available in `assets/md/` and the images are available in `assets/img/`. The homepage (main page) is `assets/md/homepage.md`.

This task is about building a static website from the `assets/md` and `assets/img` into `assets/build`. The website resulting in `assets/build` must be self sustainable.

There are some requirements:

- The design must be simple and clean.
- The design must focus on the readability of the text.
- The images must be in a comfortable size on the screen.
- Each page must includes the navigation bar.
- The navigation bar is composed by the items below:
  - "Accueil" to go back to the content of `homepage.md`
  - "Prémisses" to go to the content of `page_02.md`
  - "Plans et armées" to go to the content of `page_03.md`
  - "Opérations" to go to the content of `page_04.md`
  - "Batailles" to go to the content of `page_05.md`
  - "Sièges" to go to the content of `page_06.md`
  - "Combats" to go to the content of `page_07.md`
  - "Epilogue" to go to the content of `page_08.md`
  - "Annexes" to go to the content of `page_09.md`
  - "Crédits" to go to the content of `page_10.md`
- Each page must includes an header mentioning "Sambre-Marne-Yser" and "Août - Novembre 1914".
